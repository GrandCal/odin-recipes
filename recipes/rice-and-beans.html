Rice and Beans Recipe

Description

Rice and beans is a classic dish that is both delicious and nutritious. It is a great source of protein, fiber, and complex carbohydrates. This recipe is a simple and easy way to make rice and beans, and it can be customized to your liking by adding different vegetables, spices, or meats.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup rice, rinsed and drained
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 cups water

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper and cook for 1 minute more.
  4. Add the rice and beans and stir to combine.
  5. Pour in the water and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until the rice and beans are cooked through.
  7. Fluff with a fork and serve.

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use chicken or vegetable broth instead of water.
  • Add your favorite vegetables to the pot, such as chopped tomatoes, corn, or bell peppers.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley before serving.

This recipe is a great way to cook a delicious and healthy meal. It is also very affordable and easy to make. Enjoy!
