Omelet Recipe

Description

An omelet is a savory dish made from beaten eggs quickly cooked in a frying pan, sometimes folded around a filling such as cheese, vegetables, or meat. Omelets can be served for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Steps

  1. Whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Heat the butter or oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook for a few seconds until the bottom begins to set.
  4. Using a spatula, gently lift the edges of the omelet and tilt the skillet so that the uncooked egg mixture can flow underneath.
  5. Continue cooking until the omelet is set on the bottom and cooked through.
  6. Add your desired fillings to one half of the omelet.
  7. Fold the omelet in half and slide it onto a plate.
  8. Serve immediately with your favorite toppings, such as salsa, sour cream, or avocado.

Tips

  • For a fluffier omelet, add a splash of water or milk to the egg mixture.
  • Be careful not to overcook the omelet, or it will become dry and tough.
  • You can add any toppings you like to your omelet. Some popular toppings include cheese, vegetables, and meat.
  • Omelets can be served for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Enjoy!
